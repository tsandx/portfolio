To Reach Gen-Z Students, Colleges Meet Them Where They Are — on TikTok

An in-depth profile of how UF became a collegiate TikTok trailblazer under Sanders' leadership — from pouring Gatorade on a sidewalk to get the perfect reflection shot, to building a "TikTok army" of student creators and having a university president become the first university president on TikTok.

Wired

Colleges Need Influencers, but Do Influencers Need College?

Sanders is quoted as a leading practitioner on the philosophy of authentic social media — explaining why UF deliberately avoids co-opting student content for recruitment, and why organic community storytelling is more valuable than polished official content.

"Ninety-nine percent of the posts on social about the University of Florida are from people who are not on my team. We cherish their creations more than our 'official' content because it's authentic. To take that pureness and alter it in any way for recruitment purposes, at least to me, taints it." — Todd Sanders, quoted in Wired

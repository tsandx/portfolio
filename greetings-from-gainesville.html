Campaign · University of Florida · 2017

Greetings from Gainesville

A postcard activation that turned move-in week into a moment of global connection — reaching across 41 states and 34 countries.

Role   Director of Social Media
Platform   On-campus + social amplification
Org   University of Florida

The goal

Capture the emotion of a new semester and humanize the UF brand — not through a campaign about the university, but through one about the people who love students enough to help them get there.

What we did

The UF Social Street Team set up a postcard giveaway during move-in week, handing out custom-designed "Greetings from Gainesville, Florida" postcards. Students were invited to write messages to anyone who helped them reach this moment — parents, grandparents, favorite teachers, faraway friends, little brothers, long-distance partners.

Over 1,200 students stopped, sat down, and wrote something real. The results were tracked, mapped, and shared — revealing a story that stretched far beyond campus.

The win

1,200+ postcards written
41 states reached
34 countries across 6 continents
10 languages written

Written in 10 languages, the messages became a powerful reminder of the global community UF attracts — and the deeply personal journeys behind every student. The campaign became a model for future community-driven activations and was widely shared across social channels.
